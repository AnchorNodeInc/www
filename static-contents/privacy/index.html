4. Cookies and similar technologies
Cookies are small digital files that are automatically generated upon visiting a website and are stored on your device.

We authorize external entities, including analytics providers, advertising networks, and supplementary advertising service providers, to employ cookies and comparable technologies for the acquisition of data concerning your interactions with applications and your utilization of services across diverse devices. These third parties may exploit this data for the purpose of presenting advertisements on our Services and other online platforms, spanning a variety of websites and services, finely tailored to align with your inclinations, preferences, and attributes. You possess the option to govern the utilization and disclosure of information for these objectives.

By accessing our website or employing our services, you express consent with our utilization of cookies, as harmonized with the provisions laid out in this Privacy Policy. It is feasible to deactivate cookies through the settings of your web browser or mobile device; however, this course of action will result in the compromise of the complete functionality of our services.

a) Required Cookies: Our deployment of security-centric cookies serves the purpose of safeguarding our services. These Security and Service Integrity Cookies may, among other functions, facilitate the identification of potential fraudulent or abusive activities, the enforcement of our Terms of Service and End User License Agreement, and the assurance of user safety. These cookies are obligatory and cannot be blocked if you intend to use the service.

b) Performance and Operational Cookies: We engage analytics and research cookies to gather insights about how visitors interact with our services. This includes aggregated data such as the overall count of service users and the types of devices used. Such information may be employed to generate anonymized, consolidated reports and to contribute to service enhancement. Feature tracking cookies are also utilized to obtain information about service performance, subsequently informing improvements and generating reports.

c) Advertising Cookies: Advertising cookies are leveraged to personalize the advertisements exhibited to you within the Service and on third-party platforms.

d) Other entities placing advertisements on our websites, games, and/or applications may also employ cookies to procure information about you upon interacting with or clicking on advertisements. We bear no jurisdiction over the actions of these third parties and are unable to assure compliance with comparable privacy and security protocols as our own. Any inquiries regarding the usage of collected data by them should be addressed directly to these entities.

 

5. Exercising your preferences

Anchor Node affords you a range of alternatives for governing the utilization and sharing of your data, including:

Email Correspondence: You hold the prerogative to opt out of receiving commercial email communications from us. To have this take into effect, you are to adhere to the unsubscribe instructions furnished in our email messages. It's important to acknowledge that even if you opt out of commercial email messages, you may still receive notifications pertaining to your account, transactions, and analogous service-related messages.

Geolocation Information: Your device's functionalities may permit you to manage the collection and potential sharing of precise geolocation data with third parties through your device settings. It should be noted that opting out of supplying precise geolocation information might necessitate adjustments to various settings, not exclusively those related to location permissions. Opting out of providing exact geolocation information may lead to the deduction of your approximate location from your IP address and other amassed data. Refer to your device settings for further insights.

 

Cookies and Comparable Technologies: You may have the capacity to decline or deactivate cookies by configuring your web browser preferences. Given the diversity in web browsers, consult the guidelines provided by your browser (typically under the "help" section). Additionally, you might need to undertake supplementary steps to disable or disallow local storage and akin technologies. For instance, Adobe Local Shared Objects can be managed through the instructions accessible on Adobe's Setting Manager page. Be aware that the removal of cookies from your browser might, in certain instances, revoke your preferences for interest-based advertising. Electing to decline, deactivate, or erase cookies and similar technologies could potentially curtail some functionalities of the Services.

Do Not Track Signals and Analogous Mechanisms: Specific web browsers may dispatch "do-not-track" signals to websites with which the user engages. Presently, Anchor Node does not implement actions in response to such signals.

Interest-Based Advertising: Although an entire cessation of advertising is unfeasible, you retain the authority to regulate the receipt of interest-based ads contingent on data amassed or received regarding your online activities and interests across various devices and unaffiliated sites and services. Opting out of interest-based ads furnished by entities engaged in self-regulatory initiatives, such as the Network Advertising Initiative (US), the Digital Advertising Alliance (US), EDAA (EU), and DAAC (Canada), can be executed by adhering to the directives outlined on their respective websites. If you're using a mobile application, you may also need to opt out of interest-based advertising via your mobile device or app. Consult your device and app settings for additional guidance. It's important to note that your opt-out selection is applicable solely to the browser and device in use when you enact the opt-out process.

Additional Permissions for Games and Devices: The ability to configure privacy settings for push notifications, address book sharing, and other features, both within the Services and through your device permissions, might be at your disposal. To terminate information collection by an Anchor Node game, you can uninstall the game using the standard uninstallation procedure offered by your device or the applicable app marketplace or network.
